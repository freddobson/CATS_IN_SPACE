# Sprite Guide - "To the Death Star and Back" - Gizmo's Revenge!

## Player Sprite
- **player_gizmo.svg** - Gizmo, the brave black cat spaceship with yellow eyes and green thrusters

## Enemy Sprites - Cat Toys!

### Small Enemies (1 HP)
- **enemy_mouse.svg** - Toy Mouse (50 points, fast)
- **enemy_feather.svg** - Feather Toy (80 points, colorful)

### Medium Enemies (2 HP)
- **enemy_yarn.svg** - Ball of Yarn (100 points, tangled)
- **enemy_catnip.svg** - Catnip Pouch (120 points, intoxicating)

### Boss Enemy (3 HP)
- **enemy_laser.svg** - Laser Pointer BOSS (200 points, can capture with beam!)

## Power-Up Sprites - Cat Treats!
- **powerup_treat.svg** - Kibble Treat (standard power-up)
- **powerup_fish.svg** - Fish Treat (special power-up)
- **powerup_heart.svg** - Heart (extra life!)

## Wave Progression (10 Waves Total)

1. **Wave 1** - Toy Mice Invasion (mice + feathers)
2. **Wave 2** - Feather Toys (feathers + mice)
3. **Wave 3** - Balls of Yarn Appear! (yarn introduced)
4. **Wave 4** - Mixed Formation (catnip introduced)
5. **Wave 5** - First Laser Pointer Boss!
6. **Wave 6** - Yarn & Catnip Assault
7. **Wave 7** - Double Laser Pointer Danger!
8. **Wave 8** - All Enemy Types Mixed
9. **Wave 9** - Catnip Madness
10. **Wave 10** - FINAL ASSAULT (Triple Laser Pointer Boss!)

## Enemy Abilities

Only **Laser Pointer** enemies can use the capture beam attack (just like the boss in classic Galaga). When captured by the beam, Gizmo loses a life and respawns, but a ghostly captured-Gizmo follows the laser pointer until you destroy it!

## Theme

The entire game is cat-themed:
- **Player**: Gizmo the heroic black bombay cat defending Earth
- **Enemies**: Evil cat toys invading from space
- **Bosses**: The mesmerizing laser pointers
- **Power-ups**: Delicious cat treats as rewards
